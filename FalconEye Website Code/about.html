About the App

Purpose

    As Upper Classmen at Foothill High School in Pleasanton, We at FalconEye wanted to improve the campus navigation experience on campus for the incoming Freshmen as well as parents who don't visit the campus often.

Our Design process

    We began our App by designing each of the individual buildings that exist on campus using a software called Blender. We then proceeded to connect those buildings to form our campus through another software called Unity, which we used to code interactions with the app, and implement other development kits such as ARKit and Vuforia.
