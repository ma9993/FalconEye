About This App

Purpose

On the first day of school, many students get lost and arrive at their class way after the bell rings. ScholAR is an augmented reality based service that allows students to traverse their school campus without leaving the comfort of their home.

Features

Students will be able to map out their own routes and find the most time efficient way to get to their classrooms. Lost at campus? Pull up ScholAR, type in the classroom number, and follow the line to your classroom.
